ADA Compliance Statement for Fuel-Cards.net

Fuel-Cards.net is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all individuals, including those with disabilities. We continuously work to improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards to our website.

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Our goal is to make Fuel-Cards.net accessible to a broad audience, regardless of ability or technology. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website by adhering to best practices and standards defined by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).

Measures to Support Accessibility

To ensure accessibility, we have taken the following measures:

  • Ongoing Testing: We regularly test our website to ensure compliance with WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, addressing issues as they arise.
  • Accessible Design: Our website is designed to be compatible with common screen readers and other assistive technologies.
  • User Feedback: We encourage and welcome feedback from all users on accessibility issues, as it helps us to identify areas for improvement.

Accessibility Features

Some of the specific accessibility features on our website include:

  • Text Alternatives: We provide text alternatives for non-text content, such as images and icons, to ensure users can understand all content.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Our website is designed to be navigable via keyboard for users who are unable to use a mouse.
  • Consistent Layout: Our content is organized in a clear, straightforward layout to support ease of navigation for all users.

Ongoing Improvements

We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing process, and we are dedicated to continuous improvement. Our team is regularly reviewing and updating our website to enhance accessibility and usability for all users.

Contact Us

If you encounter any accessibility barriers while using Fuel-Cards.net or have suggestions on how we can improve our accessibility efforts, please reach out to us. We are here to help and committed to providing you with the assistance you need.
